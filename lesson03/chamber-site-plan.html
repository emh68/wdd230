Site Name:

This site plan was originally for the Draper City Chamber of Commerce, in Draper, UT. I ended up changing my plan and creating a Chamber of Commerce website for Salt Lake instead. This is why the wireframe says Draper Chamber instead of Salt Lake Chamber. This name represents the city in which the chamber operates.

Site Purpose:

The Salt Lake Chamber of Commerce works to facilitate relationships and networking for businesses in the area, in order to promote growth and further economic development. The goal of this website is to:

  1. Help businesses connect with the chamber of commerce and join.
  2. Provide a central resource to help businesses know more about the city and community they are in and how they can engage more with the community.

Scenarios:

  • What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?
  • Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?
  • What programs and initiatives are available through the chamber of commerce?
