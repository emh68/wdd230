Site Name:

Draper Chamber of Commerce. This name represents a city in which the chamber operates.

Site Purpose:

The Draper Chamber of Commerce works to facilitate relationships and networking for businesses in the area, in order to promote growth and further economic development. The goal of this website is to:

  1. Help businesses connect with the chamber of commerce and join.
  2. Provide a central resource to help businesses know more about the city and community they are in and how they can engage more with the community.

Scenarios:

  • What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?
  • Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?
  • What programs and initiatives are available through the chamber of commerce?
