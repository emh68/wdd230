Fitt's Law

Instagram

instagram.com

Fitt's Law suggests that the time to reach a target is influenced by the size of the target and its distance from the starting point. Larger targets and closer targets are easier and faster to reach.Instagram's mobile app uses Fitt's Law by having easily accessible icons for actions like posting, liking, and commenting. The design emphasizes simplicity and user-friendly touch targets.

Hick's Law

Apple

apple.com

Hick's Law suggests that increasing the number of choices will also increase the decision time logarithmically. In other words reducing the number of choices can lead to faster decision-making and better user experiences. This type of simplicity and minimalism is demonstrated by Apple's clean, simple look and minimalism. The Apple website keeps things simple and shows you exactly what they have to offer, without a lot of clutter and lots of choices. Everything is minimalistic and simple.

Proximity

Amazon

amazon.com

Proximity refers to the placement of elements in close proximity to indicate a relationship. Grouping related elements together helps users perceive them as a single, unified concept. The Amazon website demonstrates this design principle by grouping related elements together on its homepage, such as product categories, to facilitate user navigation and help users quickly find what they are looking for.
