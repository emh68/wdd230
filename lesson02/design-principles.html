Fitt's Law

Instagram

Instagram

Fitt's Law suggests that the time to reach a target is influenced by the size of the target and its distance from the starting point. Larger targets and closer targets are easier and faster to reach.Instagram's mobile app uses Fitt's Law by having easily accessible icons for actions like posting, liking, and commenting. The design emphasizes simplicity and user-friendly touch targets.
