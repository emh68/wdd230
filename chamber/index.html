Our Mission

The mission of the Salt Lake Chamber of Commerce is to bring business leaders in the Salt Lake area together to build relationships, facilitate business growth and stability and improve the local economy.

Membership Benefits

Business Highlights

In addition to our ribbon cutting ceremonies, every month we highlight a business member of the chamber. You are able to introduce your business and let the community know a little more about what you do and the services you provide.

Website Advertising

We are constantly advertising the benefits of businesses in our community and how they can help drive economic growth. Not only do we list chamber members in our business directory, but we also provide dedicated advertising opportunities.

Connect

We also have special events where you can network and connect with other business leaders in the community and we have special partnerships that can help connect you with additional tools and resources to help your business thrive.
